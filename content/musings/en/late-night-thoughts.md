# A Few Thoughts Late at Night

Some things are not done because you figured them out first; they are done because you started, and then you gradually understand them.

I increasingly feel that maturity is not about flattening every emotion, but about allowing them to exist without being driven by them.

Maybe what is truly worth cherishing is not the moments that seem loud, but the ones when you can quietly stay in your own world.
